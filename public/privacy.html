Privacy Policy

Effective June 24, 2026

Origin (“the app”) is a personal supplement and medication tracker. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Account info — your email address and a display name you provide, to create and access your account.
  • Your tracking data — the supplements and medications you add, doses, schedules, protocols, and which items you’ve checked off each day.
  • A user identifier associated with your account.

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

How we use it

Solely to provide the app’s functionality — storing your account, syncing your supplements and daily logs, and showing your schedule and reminders. We do not use your data for advertising.

Where it’s stored

Your account and tracking data are stored securely with our backend provider (Supabase) over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Your sign-in token is stored locally on your device. Reminders are scheduled locally on your device and are not sent to any server.

Sharing

We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties, except our infrastructure provider (Supabase) acting solely to store and serve your data on our behalf.

Your choices

You can edit or delete your supplements and data in the app at any time. To delete your account and associated data, contact us at the email below.

Children

Origin is not directed to children under 13.
